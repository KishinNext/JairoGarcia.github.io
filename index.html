About me

Hi!, I'm Jairo an electronic engineer located in Mosquera, Colombia. It is a city near Bogota D.C., I know, it is a little far, but, I like to live in small towns. I love and enjoy to learn new technologies and apply them to beautiful and curious projects. I consider myself as a self-motivated person, who enjoys overcoming challenges.

I have participated in different projects, one of them is a system for the massive download of documents using Python to extract information using OCR (Object Character Recognition) with Pytesseract and Azure to analyze customer's behaviors, also, I worked in Exploratory data analysis (ETL's and EDA's) using Pandas and SAS, to present results information with tools like Tableau and Power BI.

Currently, I am studying front-end and back-end technologies, I have been working on different front-end challenges in Frontend mentor and personal projects, I think that combine data science, machine learning, and web applications are the current wave of technology.
